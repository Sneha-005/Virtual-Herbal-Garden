Buckthorn refers to a group of shrubs or small trees belonging to the genus Rhamnus. The most commonly known medicinal species are Rhamnus cathartica (Common BuckthRhamnus) and Rhamnus frangula (Alder Buckthorn). These plants have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for various purposes, particularly for their laxative properties.
